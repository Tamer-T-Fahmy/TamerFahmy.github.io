About Me

I am an Engineering Leader with over 20 years of experience in Tech, passionate about bridging the gap between technology and academia. Based in Dublin, Ireland, I lead global teams that connect complex technical systems with business operations, driving innovation and operational excellence at scale.

With an MBA (Distinction) from the University of Wales Trinity Saint David and a B.E. in Communication & Electronics from Ain Shams University, I combine deep technical expertise with strategic business acumen. My work focuses on driving AI transformation initiatives that enhance operational efficiency and deliver measurable business impact.

Beyond my professional role, I am passionate about giving back through mentorship, helping to unlock potential in aspiring technologists worldwide. I also contribute to the academic community through research publications on Artificial Intelligence, IT service management, and knowledge management — bridging the worlds of industry practice and scholarly research.
