Leadership Philosophy

Core principles that guide my approach to leadership and personal growth

Over 20+ years in technology leadership, I've developed a set of guiding principles that shape how I lead teams, make decisions, and approach challenges. These five principles form the foundation of my leadership philosophy.

01

Define Success

"Define success on your own terms, achieve it by your own rules, and build a life you're proud to live."

— Anne Sweeney

What does success really look like?
Start with the WHY!
Plan, Execute, Monitor & Control
Purpose Vision Goals
02

Passion

"Working hard for something you love is 'Passion' while working hard for something you don't love is 'Stress'"

People don't find their passion by What they do, they find it by Why they do it!

Fuels Motivation
Ensure Long-Term Success
Overcome the fear of failure
Motivation to Persevere
03

Focus on Impact

"Focus on impact, not motion!"

Don't confuse motion with Progress
Focus on Short & Long term Impact
Impact Dynamics
Influence × Contribution × Quantification × Complexity
04

Persistence

"The sky is not your limit... you are"

Embrace your failure
Fail Fast, Learn Faster
Move Fast (Agile = Iterative + Incremental)
Infinite-game mindset
Identify Imposter Syndrome
Develop Discipline (1% better everyday)
05

Humbleness

"Be confident and bold while also being humble"

Open for Feedback

Servant Leadership
