Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how I handle your data.

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how tamerfahmy.net ("the Website") handles information when you visit. I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about my practices.

The short version: This is a personal portfolio website. I do not collect, store, or process any personal data directly. There are no user accounts, contact forms, or analytics tracking. However, this site uses third-party services that may collect limited technical data.

Information I Collect

Information I Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: No names, email addresses, or personal details are collected through this website
  • User Accounts: There is no registration or login functionality
  • Form Data: There are no contact forms; email links use standard mailto: protocol
  • Analytics Data: I do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking scripts
  • Cookies: This website does not set any first-party cookies

Cookies

This website does not set any first-party cookies. However, third-party services (Google Fonts, Cloudflare) may set cookies for their own purposes such as:

  • Caching resources for faster loading
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Performance optimization

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking third-party cookies may affect the appearance of fonts and icons on this website.

These external sites have their own privacy policies. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

Your Rights

Under GDPR (if you're in the EU/EEA) and other privacy laws, you have rights including:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data held about you
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data

Since this website does not collect personal data directly, there is typically no data to access, delete, or modify. For data collected by third-party services, please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Privacy Statement

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 16 years of age. I do not knowingly collect any information from children.
